Overview

State of the Map 2016 is taking place from September 23rd to 25th 2016 in Brussels, Belgium. You may also be interested in the humanitarian mapping events immediatly prior to State of the Map, and the developers hack event immediatly after. Here is the outline of the events in Brussels:

  • Thursday, September 22nd: HOT Summit at the BTC Conference Center and FOSS4G Belgium at Brussels Environment and Energy Agency, followed by an Missing Maps evening mapathon (currently this mapathon is unconfirmed).
  • Friday, September 23rd: Join us for day one of State of the Map. Details of the talks are below, with additional space available for informal "Birds of a feather" meetings.
  • Saturday, September 24th: We move to day two of State of the Map. Our scheduled program of talks is below, and "Birds of a feather" sessions are possible. In the evening join us at Brussels Event Brewery for free food and drink.
  • Sunday, September 25th: The third and final day of State of the Map will focus on workshops and sharing knowledge. Together we'll discover the next great thing in mapping and hacking but please be kind to our network (bandwidth currently unconfirmed).
  • Monday, September 26th: The local geospatial and open source communities are currently looking to set up a dedicated developer hack day. (tbd)
